Introduction

Allow us to introduce Patrick Palmer, a down-to-earth musician and a valued member of our ensemble in the production of "Sweeney Todd." This marks Patrick's first step into the world of musical theatre, but his musical journey has been a heartfelt one. He's had the pleasure of contributing his talents to some unique musical acts, including "Gallows Humor," known for their quirky and darkly comedic music, and "Cutthroat Reef," a rollicking sea-shanty, pirate band.

In addition to these adventures, Patrick is also an instrumentalist in "Tea & Strumpets," a delightful vaudeville-style folk group that brings merriment to Renaissance Faires. In "Sweeney Todd," Patrick's enthusiasm shines through as he becomes an essential part of our dark and captivating production. He's passionate about music in all its forms, and he's excited to be part of this production alongside his friends. Join us in celebrating Patrick's debut in "Sweeney Todd," where his musical talents and warm-hearted approach add depth to our musical experience. Don't miss the chance to witness his musical journey as it unfolds before your eyes!

  2. Study for the musical, Sweeney Todd
